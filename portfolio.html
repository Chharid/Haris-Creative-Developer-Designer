Got Questions?

Frequently Asked

Everything you need to know before we start working together. If you have a question not answered here, just send me a message.

Ask Me Directly
How long does a typical project take?
Most web projects take 1–3 weeks depending on complexity. E-commerce stores typically 2 weeks, AI apps 1–2 weeks, branding projects 1 week. I always provide a clear timeline before starting.
Do I need hosting or a backend?
For most projects I build, you don't need a paid backend at all. I use modern browser storage and APIs to create fully functional apps. You just need basic static hosting (free on GitHub Pages, Netlify, etc.).
What is your payment structure?
I typically work with 50% upfront and 50% on delivery. For larger projects, I may split into milestones. I accept payment via bank transfer, JazzCash, or EasyPaisa.
Can you work with international clients?
Absolutely. I work remotely with clients worldwide. Communication is in English or Urdu, and I am flexible with time zones for calls and updates.
Do you offer post-launch support?
Yes — every project includes 30 days of free post-launch support for bug fixes and minor adjustments. Ongoing maintenance packages are available at a monthly rate.
Can I see more examples of your work?
Yes! Visit the Projects page for a full portfolio. You can also contact me for private case studies and source code demos. I'm always happy to show more on a call.
